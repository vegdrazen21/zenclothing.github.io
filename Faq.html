Frequently Asked Questions

We want to make your shopping experience as smooth as possible. Here are some common questions about our anime clothing line.

What sizes do your clothing items come in?

Our clothing items come in a wide range of sizes, from XS to 3XL. We include a size chart on each product page to help you select the right fit.

How do I know which anime designs are available?

Each product page displays the available designs. You can also check our "New Arrivals" section for the latest releases.

What are your shipping options?

We offer standard, expedited, and international shipping. Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your location.

Can I exchange or return my purchase?

Yes! We offer a 30-day return or exchange policy. The items must be unworn and in original condition. Please visit our "Returns & Exchanges" page for more details.

How can I contact customer support?

You can contact us through our "Contact Us" page, where we provide an email form, phone number, and social media links for customer support.

Do you offer gift cards?

Yes, we offer digital gift cards in various denominations. You can purchase them on our website and send them to your friends or family.
